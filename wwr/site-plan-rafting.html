Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to educate about the environment and water safety all while appreciating nature, providing exercise and facilitating fun in a way that will take you away from the everyday stressors of life for a time.

Audience

Who is our target audience? We are hoping that anyone from 8 to 800 that is searching for a temporary respite from the everyday stresses of life, lovers of nature and thrill seekers will enjoy our services and possibly learn something along the way.

What do our customers want? Easy planning! We have provided tools so that you can plan your next white water rafting adventure with ease. Our full service phone app has been created to take the guess work out of planning a white water rafting excursion to help enhance the excitement of your rafting adventure.Feel free to browse our website to find all the important details you need to book your trip with us using the calendar option to determine the best date to experience your adventure, pricing information that works within your budget including the many discounts we provide for church and school groups, active duty military and verterans as well as first responders. We also include interactive maps to be able to obtain detailed directions to our location as well as what the terrain looks like on your chosen excursion.

Can't find what your are looking for? Feel free to utilize our chat option with one of our customer care team members utilizing either your phone, desktop or laptop computer so that we can assist you promptly with any questions you may have. There is also a 'know before you go' section with all the suggested gear you may want to bring to enhance your experience as well as safety tips as it is of the utmost importance to us that all our guests remain safe while on one of our adventures.Also be sure to visit our online store to buy any merchandise that you may want to bring home as a reminder of your epic adventure.
