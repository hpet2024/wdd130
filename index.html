Hello! My name is Heather Peters and I am from Binghamton NY but I have lived many places. I currently live in Casselberry Florida. In my free time I enjoy doing crafty things such as cross stitch and basket weaving.

Florida

Florida is a peninsula almost completely surrounded by water. On the east coast is the Atlantic Ocean and the west coast is the Gulf of Mexico. At it's northernmost edge is the states Alabama in the northwest and Georgia in the northeast. Florida is world-renowned for its beaches, amusement parks, warm and sunny climate, and tourist attractions.
