Heather Peters | WDD 130

Hello! My name is Heather Peters and I am from Binghamton NY but I have lived many places since my father was in the military. I met my husband in the summer between sophomore and junior year in high school. We were married in July 1991 in Omaha Nebraska and sealed in the Washington DC Temple. I am currently a student at BYU-Idaho through BYU Pathway and I have learned so much so far and look forward to completing my degree in software development. I moved to Florida in December 2019 to be close to my maternal grandmother, I currently live in Casselberry Florida which is about 40 minutes north of Walt Disney World. My husband and I are ordinance workers in the Orlando Florida Temple every other Saturday and we team teach Come Follow Me in Sunday School to the 11-14 year old youth of our ward. In my free time I enjoy doing crafty things such as cross stitch and basket weaving.

Florida

Florida is a peninsula almost completely surrounded by water. On the east coast is the Atlantic Ocean and the west coast is the Gulf of Mexico. At it's northernmost edge are the states of Alabama in the northwest and Georgia in the northeast. Florida is known for its beaches, tourist attractions and it's warm and sunny climate.
